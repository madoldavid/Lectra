# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** February 10, 2026

Lectra (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides a lecture recording and notes application (“Lectra”, the “App”). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Information We Collect

### Account information
- **Email address**: used to create and authenticate your account via Supabase.

### Audio and notes (local only)
- **Audio recordings, transcripts, and notes** are stored **locally on your device**. We do **not** upload them to our servers.

### Speech recognition
- The App uses the device’s speech recognition services to generate transcripts. Depending on your device settings, audio may be processed by your device vendor (e.g., Google/Apple) to provide speech‑to‑text functionality. We do not control or receive this data.

## How We Use Information
- Authenticate and manage your account.
- Provide core app functionality (recording and transcription).
- Improve stability and performance.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell your data.

We share limited information with:
- **Supabase** (authentication provider) to handle account login.
- **Device speech recognition services** (as required by your OS) to transcribe audio.

## Data Retention
- **Local recordings and notes** remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.
- **Account data** (email) is retained as long as your account is active.

## Your Choices
- You can revoke microphone access in device settings.
- You can delete local recordings from your device.
- You can request account deletion by contacting us.

## Security
We use standard security measures to protect your account information and authentication data.

## Children’s Privacy
Lectra is not intended for children under 13.
